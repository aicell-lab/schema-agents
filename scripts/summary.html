Investigating the Role of mmu_circ_26984 in Aniline-Induced Hepatotoxicity

Materials:
  • Aniline
  • N-acetylcysteine (NAC)
  • AML12 cells
  • C57BL/6 mice
  • RNA extraction kits
  • RT-qPCR reagents
  • Western blot materials
  • Immunohistochemistry kits
  • Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH) probes
  • CircRNA microarray kits
Protocol:
  • Treat AML12 cells and C57BL/6 mice with aniline to induce hepatotoxicity.
  • Administer NAC to a subset of aniline-treated cells and mice to assess its protective effect.
  • Perform circRNA microarray analysis to identify differentially expressed circRNAs.
  • Use RNA pulldown, LC-MS, and RNA immunoprecipitation to study the interaction between mmu_circ_26984 and Myh9.
  • Conduct RT-qPCR, Western blot, and immunohistochemistry to analyze gene and protein expression.
  • Apply FISH to visualize the localization of mmu_circ_26984 in liver tissues.

Assessment of Hematological Biomarkers for Lead Poisoning Prediction

Materials:
  • Human blood samples
  • Lead standards
  • Hematological analyzer
  • Logistic regression software
  • ROC curve analysis software
Protocol:
  • Collect blood samples from individuals with occupational lead poisoning and healthy controls.
  • Analyze hematological parameters using a hematological analyzer.
  • Develop a logistic regression model incorporating RDW-CV, NLR, and Micro% to predict lead poisoning.
  • Evaluate the diagnostic value of the model using ROC curve analysis.
  • Compare the sensitivity and specificity of the model to existing diagnostic methods.

Elucidating the Impact of Pb and As on NRF2-Target Genes in Neural Progenitor Cells

Materials:
  • Human neural progenitor cells (NPCs)
  • Lead (Pb) and arsenic (As) compounds
  • ChIP-qPCR assay materials
  • RNA extraction kits
  • RT-qPCR reagents
  • Western blot materials
  • Transcriptomic analysis software
Protocol:
  • Expose human NPCs to Pb and As to induce oxidative stress.
  • Perform ChIP-qPCR assays to confirm NRF2 binding to the promoter regions of F2RL2, TRIM16L, and PANX2.
  • Analyze gene expression changes using RT-qPCR.
  • Assess protein levels with Western blot.
  • Compare transcriptomic profiles of NPCs from ASD patients with those exposed to Pb and As.
